# Why Am I Talking About This?
* I joined Webs in 2013, Idris talked lots about Bitcoin
* Idris sold me Bitcoin for $250<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* After a week, I sold my Bitcoin for $125 (Lost 50% of my investment)<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* HORAY!<!-- .element class="fragment" -->


## Fast Forward to 2016
* The story came up again and I checked Bitcoin price was over $700!
* Bought a little bit of Bitcoin, by February 2017 it was up to $985!<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* Noticed this other "Ethereum" thing, it's price had been pretty steady<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* Why not, bought some of it at $11...<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* During the Design Sprint it jumped to $16, so I sold some at 40%!<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
* That was just the beginning...<!-- .element class="fragment" -->


## Ethereum's Rise
* On March 26th, Ethereum hit $50, and I was hooked
* Had started following Etherum on <a target="_blank" href="http://www.cryptocompare.com/">https://www.cryptocompare.com</a>
* Noticed other coins, asked my friend about it, he mentioned Monero, Ripple, and Litecoin<!-- .element class="fragment" -->


## March 29th 2017
* Did all kinds of digging to find ways to buy those coins.
* <!-- .element class="fragment" -->Bought Monero by transfering BTC through <a target="_blank" href="https://shapeshift.io/#/coins">shapshift.io</a> (ripoff) and transfered to <a target="_blank" href="https://mymonero.com/#/">MyMonero.com</a> Wallet (weird/shady)
* <!-- .element class="fragment" -->Bought Ripple by sending ETH to <a target="_blank" href="https://gatehub.net/">GateHub.net</a> which was the single most confusing site I've ever used for money.
* <!-- .element class="fragment" -->Eventaully found <a target="_blank" href="https://www.bitstamp.net/">BitStamp.net</a> which didn't have many features but was comprehendable.
* <!-- .element class="fragment" -->Finally found <a target="_blank" href="https://www.bitfinex.com/">Bitfinex.com</a> which was a really nice site. Was waiting for ETH to transfer over so I could buy Litecoin when...
* <!-- .element class="fragment" -->My FiOS went out!  There was an outage, I wated a couple hours, then went to sleep.


## March 30th 2017
* I woke up the next morning and Litecoin went from 4.5 to 9!
  * Litecoin had spent 2 full years hovering between 3 and 4.5 before that<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
!["BitcoinPriceChart"](cryptos-talk-05-10-2017/images/Litecoin-PriceHistory.png)<!-- .element class="fragment" -->
